Home

Our website, "SHEIN", will provide a centralized platform where users can find the best local deals and discounts in their area. The platform will feature offers from women and the local dealers.A variety of categories in shopping, entertainment,selling and services. By aggregating local deals, we aim to help users save money while driving traffic and sales to local businesses.

Market Analysis

The SHEIN is growing as consumers increasingly seek value for their money. With the rise of e-commerce and the digital economy, there is a significant opportunity to capture a portion of this by providing an easy-to-use platform that highlights local deals with our women. Our target audience includes budget-conscious consumers, families, students, and young professionals and WOMEN too.

Business Models

Our revenue models will include:

  • Advertising: Local businesses by women can pay to have their deals featured prominently on our website.
  • Marketing: Earning commissions from partner businesses for deals purchased through our SHEIN platform.
  • Subscription Services: Offering premium memberships for users to access exclusive deals and discounts.

Marketing Strategy

Our marketing strategy will focus on:

  • Social Media Marketing: Leverage platforms like Facebook, Whatsapp,Instagram, and Twitter to reach our target audience especially woman.
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Optimize our website to rank highly on search engines for relevant keywords.
  • Partnerships: Collaborate with local influencers, bloggers, woman entrepreneurs to promote our platform.
  • Local Advertising: Use flyers, local newspapers, FM radio to reach potential users in specific regions.
